Scholars' Gazette is a student run platform which encourages youth's active and vigilant participation in discussing and finding productive solutions for social issues at different levels. The Scholars' Gazette's platforms like the monthly and annual newsletters and the Blog shall keep the readers updated with current affairs and other topics of national and international importance. Young Scholars program of the Scholars' Gazette is the medium where students could show there extraordinary writing skills by submitting their daily beat for the blog or their articles to be published for the monthly and annual newsletters.
